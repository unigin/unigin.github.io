What is Bukkit Unigin?

Bukkit Unigin is an application that allows you to create bukkit plugins with no programming knowledge. Its still in Alpha, so there may be some bugs and missing feauters. If you notice any of these, contact us and we will try to fix it. Have fun and don't let anything stop you from making the plugins in your dreams.
